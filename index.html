NYC Citi Bike 2023 Ridership Analysis

Built a streaming Python pipeline that processes all 35.1 million Citi Bike trips from 2023 — 6.8 GB of nested ZIP/CSV data — entirely in memory, never writing the raw files to disk. The result is a single, dependency-free interactive dashboard surfacing commuter patterns, seasonality, and the electric-bike transition (50% → 62% over the year).
